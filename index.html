A tech fest, short for technology festival, is an event that showcases various technological innovations and advancements. It typically features a range of activities such as presentations, exhibitions, workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities for participants to learn and interact with others in the technology industry. Tech fests are often organized by educational institutions, technology companies, or community groups with an interest in promoting technology and innovation.

They provide a platform for individuals and businesses to showcase their ideas, products, and services and can be a great opportunity for networking and collaboration in the technology industry
